Introduction

At Elite Financial Consulting Office, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our clients’ data and website visitors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information. If we make any significant changes to this policy, we will notify you (for example via email or through a notice on our website).

Our Values

Your information belongs to you. We only collect the necessary information to provide our accounting and tax services. When this information is no longer needed, we delete it or anonymize it.

We protect your information. We do not share your data with any third party without your permission, unless we are legally required to do so.

We help our clients protect their privacy. We are committed to providing accounting and consulting services in a way that ensures the highest level of confidentiality, and we take all necessary measures to protect our clients’ data.

Why do we process your information?

We process your data for the following reasons:

To provide the accounting, tax, and consulting services you request.

To respond to your inquiries and provide necessary support.

To improve our services based on customer feedback.

To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

To manage client files and prepare periodic reports.

We always strive to balance our legitimate interests with protecting your privacy and rights.

Your Rights Regarding Your Data

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

Request access to your personal data.

Correct or update your data.

Request deletion of your data if there is no legal need to retain it.

Object to the use of your data for marketing or statistical purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, you can contact us through the contact methods below.

Where do we store your data?

Your data may be processed inside or outside your country. We take all necessary measures to ensure that your data is protected under appropriate security standards.

Data retention period We retain your data only for the period necessary to achieve the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. After that, the information is securely deleted or anonymized.

How do we protect your data?

We use advanced security technologies and procedures to ensure the protection of your data from loss, unauthorized use, or illegal access. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Use of Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your browsing experience on our website. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect some site functions.
